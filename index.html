about us

the highest quality
at slow wine bar

The first natural wine bar in Limburg! We are guests at ZUPPA in Hasselt. In the summer months you can enjoy natural wines on the cozy terrace. A wide range of wines, cocktails, traditional Geuzen beers, a tasty lager, coffee and tea, or a fresh glass of fruit juice. Delicious sharing dishes with fresh sourdough bread and focaccia baked by Jos, artisan cheeses and charcuterie or canned fish. Tasty and everything organic.
